Razer and Miyu have taken to the sweet life a little too easily. It's not too long before they start losing their edge, and finding a lot of extra pounds. By the time trouble rolls around again, they're nearly unrecognizable! Can the two round rangers save the day once again, or will their fattened state be their own downfall? 

A very fun part 2 I worked on with anonymous! We decided to skip the chubby phase of these two adventurers and go straight to the big ol' FAT phase. Not to worry, my dear readers, their weight is only going up from here~ 

10.9k words! I hope you all enjoy!
